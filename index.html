⚙ The platform

Every part of school operations, in one system.

Admissions to alumni. Fees to final results. EduTrack replaces the spreadsheets, paper ledgers, and disconnected apps with one connected, real‑time system your whole school runs on.

Admin command center

Manage students, staff, classes, and timetables from a single dashboard built for principals and registrars who need answers fast.

Fees & payments

Generate invoices, track instalments, and reconcile payments automatically. Parents pay online; your bursar stops chasing bank tellers.

Results & report cards

Teachers input scores once. EduTrack computes grades, ranks, and generates print‑ready report cards in every term, instantly.

Attendance & timetables

Daily attendance, automated absence alerts to parents, and timetable scheduling that adapts as classes and staff change.

Parent & student portals

Parents see results, attendance, and fee history in real time. Students log in for assignments and announcements — no app installs.

Insights & reporting

Enrollment trends, revenue forecasts, and academic performance dashboards that give school owners boardroom‑ready numbers.
